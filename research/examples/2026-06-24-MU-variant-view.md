## 5. OUR VARIANT VIEW — skew DOWN into the print (negative near-term reaction asymmetry), despite a structurally bullish business
The company's own FQ3 guide ($19.15 EPS / $35.5B rev) already brackets/exceeds street EPS (~$19.72), and 30d revisions plus a wall of $1,300–1,625 PT hikes mean **the buy-side bar is now ahead of the official guide** — classic "good number isn't good enough." The tape already **faded ~10–13% off 6/22 highs into the event**, positioning nervous, implied move above norm, consensus unanimous Buy (marginal incremental bull is scarce).
- **Confirms (down):** in-line/small beat, GM merely *meeting* 80%, no FQ4 raise → sell-the-news.
- **Refutes (up):** clean beat **+ explicit FQ4 guide-up** + GM through 80% with HBM ASP/mix commentary → re-rating extends.
- **Offset:** genuinely cheap **~10x fwd P/E vs ~31x peers** caps medium-term downside — this is a near-term *reaction* skew, not a fundamental short.
